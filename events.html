08 March : 11 am – 5pm

TCET SHOW

The "Talent Show," organized by the International Society for Technology in Education (ISTE), served as a platform for first-year engineering students to exhibit their diverse talents encompassing singing, dancing, poetry, musical instrument performances, and stand-up comedy. Held during the second week of the academic year, this cultural event provided an opportunity for students to showcase their artistic abilities, fostering a sense of community and cultural appreciation within the college environment. Through this event, participants were able to demonstrate their creativity and expressiveness while promoting a vibrant campus culture.

09 April 2024

Fiesta

The "Fiesta," orchestrated by the International Society for Technology in Education (ISTE), was an immersive cultural event tailored for first-year engineering students in auditorium. Embracing a traditional dress code, this event celebrated diversity and heritage through two distinct time slots. Participants engaged in an array of cultural activities, including traditional dance performances, culinary showcases, cultural exhibitions, and interactive workshops. Through "Fiesta," students were encouraged to embrace and appreciate different cultural perspectives, fostering cross-cultural understanding and unity within the academic community. This event provided an enriching platform for students to explore and celebrate their cultural identities while forging lasting connections with peers from diverse backgrounds.

20 May 2024

Matrubhasha Divas

"Matrubhasha Divas," organized by the TSDW in seminar Hall 1, served as a poignant celebration of our mother tongue, Hindi. Through captivating displays of dance, poetry, songs, and other artistic expressions, participants reverently showcased the beauty and richness of the Hindi language. This cultural extravaganza provided a platform for students to honor their linguistic heritage while fostering a deeper appreciation for Hindi literature and culture. Held with reverence and pride, "Matrubhasha Divas" exemplified the importance of preserving and promoting linguistic diversity, promoting a sense of belonging and cultural pride among attendees.

— TSDW
